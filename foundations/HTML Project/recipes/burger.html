Burger

Description

Burger is a juicy beef patty in a bun with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and sauces.

Grilled for smoky flavor, it's a quick meal favorite.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 4 buns
  • 4 cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Form patties from beef.
  2. Grill 4 mins each side.
  3. Assemble in buns with toppings.
