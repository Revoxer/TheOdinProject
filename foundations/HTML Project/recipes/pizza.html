Pizza

Description

Pizza has thin crust, sauce, cheese, and toppings baked crispy.

Homemade version rivals takeout with fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Veggies optional

Steps

  1. Roll dough.
  2. Add sauce, toppings.
  3. Bake 10 mins hot oven.
