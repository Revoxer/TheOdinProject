Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian layered pasta bake with rich meat ragu, creamy white sauce, and cheese, baked until bubbly.

Comforting and flavorful, it's perfect for family meals.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400g tomatoes
  • 500ml b√©chamel
  • 200g mozzarella
  • 50g parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook meat, onion, garlic; add tomatoes, simmer.
  2. Layer pasta, sauce, cheese in dish.
  3. Bake 40 mins at 180C.
